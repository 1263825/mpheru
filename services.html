Our Services


Bitumen Distributors(Sprayers)

We use highly efficient distributors that are properly maintained, which are operated by highly experienced team to give you piece of mind with every job

Plant Hire

We offer an extensive range of plant machinery to suit any given task with expert advice & on call maintenance support

Supplier of hot mix asphalt

We supply a wide variety of asphalt products for all types of roads from short access roads to municipal streets, provincial proclaimed roads and national freeways and highways

Soil stabilising
